Frequently asked questions

What does Black Seven actually do? −
Black Seven is a holding company behind a small group of operating businesses — currently an advisory division (Consultancy) and a residential property division (Casa) — each run on its own terms, under one name and one standard.
How do you determine the right strategy? +
Careful diagnosis before action: we spend time inside the business with the team, the data and the decisions before any recommendation is made, then build a practical, sequenced plan around the constraints actually in play.
How long does a typical engagement take? +
It depends on the work — a focused diagnostic can run weeks, while transformation and delivery work often spans quarters. We stay through execution rather than handing over a document.
Do you work with businesses in any industry? +
We work with businesses, entrepreneurs and investors across industries. What matters is the point of change: scaling past the original structure, preparing for investment or exit, or needing an outside view.
What kind of property does Casa invest in? +
Residential property selected for durable demand and long-term income potential — chosen on fundamentals over a full hold period, not just at the point of purchase.
How do I get in touch? +
Use the form above or email info@blackseven.co directly — advisory and property enquiries both reach the right team.
